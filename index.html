My Journey in Life Skills & Empowerment

With a passion for unlocking human potential and a rich career spanning over 19 years, I have dedicated myself to empowering individuals and organisations. My journey has taken me from a counselling Institution in Thrissur to directing RICH, all with one goal: to provide practical strategies that foster a renewed sense of purpose.

I believe in interactive sessions that don't just teach, but inspire genuine change. My work with esteemed organisations like Sahodaya, KRSMA and ICI has reinforced my commitment to delivering valuable insights that resonate long after the workshop is over.
