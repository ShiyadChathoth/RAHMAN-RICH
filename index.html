Meet Abdul Rahman

My Journey in Empowerment

With a passion for unlocking human potential and a rich career spanning over 18 years, I have dedicated myself to empowering individuals and organizations. My journey has taken me from counseling units in Thrissur to directing RICH, all with one goal: to provide practical strategies that foster a renewed sense of purpose.

I believe in interactive sessions that don't just teach, but inspire genuine change. My work with esteemed organizations like KRSMA and IAME has reinforced my commitment to delivering valuable insights that resonate long after the workshop is over.
